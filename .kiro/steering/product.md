# PersonaCraft Product Overview

PersonaCraft is an AI-powered marketing persona generation platform that creates ultra-realistic, culturally-grounded personas in under 60 seconds.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Hybrid AI Technology**: Combines Google Gemini LLM with Qloo Taste AI™ for authentic cultural intelligence
- **Speed**: Generate detailed personas in under 60 seconds
- **Authenticity**: Real cultural data including music, movies, brands, and lifestyle preferences
- **Professional Output**: Export capabilities in PDF, CSV, and JSON formats
- **Analytics Integration**: Quality scores, trends, and optimization insights

## Target Users
- Marketing professionals
- Digital agencies
- Product managers
- UX researchers
- Brand strategists

## Key Features
- Marketing brief to persona transformation
- Cultural data integration (music, movies, brands)
- Professional export formats
- Quality metrics and analytics
- Modern responsive UI
- Multi-language support (French primary, English secondary)

## Business Model
- Freemium approach with professional exports
- API integrations for enterprise users
- Focus on conversion optimization (+47% mentioned in marketing)

## Brand Positioning
- Premium AI marketing tool
- Cultural intelligence focus
- Professional-grade outputs
- Speed and authenticity combined
